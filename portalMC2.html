-STROKE-

APIXABAN

Description:

Apixaban is used to help prevent strokes or blood clots in people who have atrial fibrillation that is not caused by heart valve disease.

Function:

It is an anticoagulant prescribe to lower the risk of stroke.

Usual Dosage (for adults):


5 mg tablet taken orally, twice a day.

Common Side effects:


-bleeding gums
-nosebleeds
-heavy vaginal bleeding
-red, pink, or brown urine
-red or black, tarry stools
