-ALLERGY-

CETRIZINE

Description:

Cetirizine is an antihistamine used for the relief of symptoms of allergy like sneezing, runny nose, and watery eyes. It is also used to treat skin allergies that cause rash and itching. It works by blocking H1 (Histamine- 1) receptors from reaching its target, thereby decreasing your body's response to allergens.

Function:

prevents and treats allergy symptoms, such as red, itchy eyes, sneezing, a runny or stuffy nose, or hives.

USUAL DOSAGE: Usual Dosage:


-Adults: One 10 mg tablet once a day. Do not exceed the maximum recommended daily dose which is 20 mg.
-6 years old and up: One 10 mg tablet once a day as a single or divided dose.
-2-5 years old: 5 mL once a day or 2.5 mL twice a day (every 12 hours).
-6-12 years old: 10 mL once a day or 5 mL twice a day (every 12 hours).
-12 months-2 years old: 1 mL once or twice a day (every 12 hours).
-2-5 years old: 2 mL once a day or 1 mL twice a day (every 12 hours).
-6-12 months old: 1 mL once a day.
