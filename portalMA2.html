-HYPERTENSION-

NITROPRUSSIDE: For emergency medication in Hypertension

Action:

Nitroprusside is used predominantly to treat hypertensive emergencies, such as to lower blood pressure during acute aortic dissection or to improve cardiac output in severe congestive heart failure. Nitroprusside is unstable and must be administered intravenously with careful monitoring.

Indications and Dosages:

Nitropress - 25mg/mL (50mg/2ml vial)
Nipride RTU - 0.2mg/mL (20mg/100mL 0.9% NaCI)
Nipride RTU - 0.5mg/mL (50mg/100mL 0.9% NaCI)

Administration:

IV infusion

Common Side Effects:

-Unusual tiredness
-Chest pain or discomfort
-Dizziness
-Trouble breathing
-Fainting

Serious side effects:

-The white of your eyes turn yellow
-You get severe stomach pain
-You feel weak, have an irregular heartbeat, pins and needles and muscle cramps
