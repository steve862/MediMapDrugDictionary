AMOXICILLIN

Description:

Amoxicillin, an aminopenicillin, was created by adding an extra amino group to penicillin in order to fight antibiotic resistance.

Function and Usual Dosage:


-Amoxicillin is used to treat a wide range of bacterial infections, including tonsillitis, bronchitis, pneumonia, and infections of the skin, ear, nose, throat, and urinary tract
-Proper dosage: 250 mg orally every 8 hrs or 500 mg every 12 hrs

Common Side Effects:


-Severe stomach pain
-Rash
-Diarrhea
