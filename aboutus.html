ABOUT US

MediMap is a website designed to provide a comprehensive database of 100 commonly used medications based on the ten prevalent diseases in the Philippines. The drugs will be organized by disease to allow users to easily view the drug’s basic information and function, check its side effects, and note its intake frequency and interval. MediMap will be made available for the general public and novice healthcare teams. A precaution stating that the website is not made to advertise self-medication and clinical consultation is still advised will be shown to make sure every one’s safety. This proposal does not cover all medicines accessible in the market; only the 100 most commonly used drugs will be focused.
