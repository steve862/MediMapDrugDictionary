CEPHALEXIN

Description:

The beta-lactam antibiotic cephalexin is a member of the first-generation cephalosporin family and is available as a generic drug or as a brand-name drug that is used to treat bacterial infections like UTI.

Function and Usual Dosage:


-Cephalexin functions by obstructing the bacterial cell wall's development.
-Proper dosage: 500 mg orally every 12 hrs

Common Side Effects:


-Diarrhea
-Stomach pain
-Allergic reactions like swelling of face, lips, and throat
