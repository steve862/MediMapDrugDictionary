-STROKE-

ALTEPLASE

Description:

Alteplase is a type of tissue plasminogen activator (tPA) used in the emergency treatment of ischemic stroke, myocardial infarction, and pulmonary emboli.

Function:

It is used to break up a clot that is causing the stroke.

Usual Dosage (for adults):


0.9 mg/kg IV; not to exceed 90 mg total dose; administer 10% of the total dose as an initial IV bolus over 1 minute and the remainder infused over 60 minutes.

Common Side effects:


-Bleeding from puncture sites and wounds
-bleeding gums
-coughing up blood
-difficulty with breathing or swallowing
-dizziness
-headache
