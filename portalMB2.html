-ALLERGY-

LORATADINE (Claritin®)

Description:

tablets provide effective and long-lasting relief from a variety of allergysymptoms, letting you carry on enjoying life to the fullest without suffering from sneezing; a runny, itchy nose; itchy, watery eyes; and an itchy throat.

Function:

relieves multiple symptoms, including sneezing, runny nose, itchy & watery eyes and itchy nose or throat.

USUAL DOSAGE: Usual Dosage:


-Claritin is available as a 10 mg tablet, a 5 or 10 mg rapidly-disintegrating tablet, a 10 mg chewable tablet, and a syrup (5 mg per 5 ml). Claritin is taken once a day.
