-HYPERTENSION-

LOSARTAN: For maintenance in Hypertension

Action:

Inhibits vasoconstrictive and aldosterone secreting action of angiotensin II by blocking angiotensin II receptor on the surface of vascular smooth muscle and other tissue cell. Losartan is used to treat high blood pressure and to help protect the kidneys from damage due to diabetes.

Indications and Dosages:

Adjust-a-dose (for all indications); In adults with mild to moderate hepatic impairment, starting dose is 25 mg once daily.
ADULTS: Initially, 50 mg PO daily. Maximum daily dose is 100 mg.
Children age 6 and older: 0.7 mg/kg (up to 50 mg) PO daily, adjusted as needed up to 1.4 mg/kg/day (maximum 100 mg)

Administration:

PO (by mouth)
-Give drug without regard for meals
-If made into suspension by pharmacist, store in refrigerator for up to 4 weeks and shake well before each use.

Common Side Effects:

-Feeling dizzy
-Headaches
-Nausea
-Pain in joint muscle

Serious side effects:

-The white of your eyes turn yellow
-You get severe stomach pain
-You feel weak, have an irregular heartbeat, pins and needles and muscle cramps
